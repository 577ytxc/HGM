Tuesday | 9am-12pm : Solution Hour

He holds the solutions to all human problems; the manifestation of this, is evident in His raising the dead to life, healing and granting salvation to perishing souls by delivering them from the Devil's bondage.

Thursday | 4:30pm - 7:30pm Holyghost Service

The Holy Ghost is the third member of the Godhead. He is a personage of spirit, without a body of flesh and bones. He is often referred to as the Spirit, the Holy Spirit, the Spirit of God, the Spirit of the Lord, or the Comforter.

Sunday | 8:00am - 10:30am Liberty Service

In Christ we have true liberty from sin and true liberty to live for God, by virtue of the Holy Spirit's regenerating work, whereby we are given a new nature to seek after Him, to please Him, and to abide in Christ.
